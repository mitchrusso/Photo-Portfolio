# PhotoViewPro Showcase Roadmap

Showcase is the public discovery loop for PhotoViewPro. It should help photographers promote selected work while making every shared image point back to PhotoViewPro.

## MVP

- Public `/showcase` page.
- Approved/featured photo grid.
- Category, topic, tag, and search filters.
- Vote button.
- Lightweight comments.
- Share button with PhotoViewPro-branded link.
- Photographer attribution and portfolio link.
- Subscriber-side “Submit to Showcase” action from a portfolio photo.
- Local prototype storage until database-backed subscriber/admin workflows are ready.

## Next

- Database tables for submissions, votes, comments, categories, tags, and moderation status.
- Subscriber submission form with category, tags, caption, location, and release confirmation.
- Admin moderation queue: Pending, Approved, Rejected, Featured.
- Per-user/session/device vote limits.
- Comment moderation and abuse reporting.
- Social Open Graph image generation for every showcase submission.
- Branded share cards with photographer name, photo title, and PhotoViewPro source.

## Later

- Weekly themes such as Night Skies, Street Portraits, Wild Coastlines, Black & White Architecture.
- Featured photographer profile pages.
- Follow photographer / notify me features.
- Email roundups for weekly winners and featured work.
- “Featured on PhotoViewPro Showcase” badges photographers can embed on their own websites.
- Leaderboards by category, week, and all-time.
- Sponsor/prize integrations for themed contests.
- Optional paid featured placement, only after trust and quality standards are clear.

## Guardrails

- Showcase supports the product; it should not turn PhotoViewPro into a generic social network.
- Portfolio display remains the core value proposition.
- Comments and voting must include moderation and abuse controls before public scale.
- Submissions should feel curated, not like an unfiltered feed.
