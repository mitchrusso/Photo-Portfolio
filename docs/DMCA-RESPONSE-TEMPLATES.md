# PhotoView.io DMCA Response Templates

Last updated: July 20, 2026

These templates support the internal procedure in `docs/DMCA-NOTICE-OPERATIONS.md`. Replace every bracketed field, preserve the complete communication in the case record, and escalate disputed or high-risk matters to qualified counsel. Do not state that content infringes copyright unless that conclusion has been approved by counsel or established by a court.

## Notify a subscriber of disabled access

Subject: `[CASE ID] Access disabled following a copyright notice`

PhotoView.io disabled public access to `[URL OR MATERIAL]` on `[DATE AND TIME]` after receiving a copyright notice. A copy of the notice is attached or reproduced below. This action is not a final determination that the material infringes copyright.

If you believe the material was removed or disabled because of mistake or misidentification, review the counter-notice requirements at https://photoview.io/copyright. A counter-notice must be sent to support@photoview.io with the subject `DMCA Counter-Notice — [CASE ID]`. A compliant counter-notice will be forwarded to the original claimant and will disclose the contact and jurisdiction information it contains.

Subject: `[CASE ID] PhotoView.io counter-notice received`
